title: "Checkout"
description: "Let customers pay and activate their own subscriptions, without manual intervention from your team."
---

When you build a self-serve product, you need a way for customers to select a plan, pay, and get access immediately. Flexprice Checkout handles this entire sequence in a single API call: no manual subscription creation, no orphaned draft records, no follow-up.

You create a Checkout Session. Flexprice generates a payment link via your connected payment provider. The customer pays. Flexprice activates the subscription and finalizes the invoice automatically.

## What Checkout is for

Checkout is designed for **B2C and self-serve flows** where:

- Customers sign up and pay independently, without a sales process
- Subscription activation should be instant, tied to successful payment
- You want failed or abandoned payments to clean up automatically, with no orphaned draft records

If you're creating subscriptions on behalf of customers from your own backend (e.g., sales-assisted, contract-driven, or internal operations), use the [Subscriptions API](/docs/subscriptions/customers-create-subscription) directly. You don't need a Checkout Session for backend-initiated flows.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a session">
    Your backend calls `POST /v1/checkout/sessions` with the customer, plan, currency, and payment provider. Flexprice creates a draft subscription and invoice, then opens a payment link with your provider.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Redirect the customer">
    The response includes a `payment_action.url`. Redirect the customer's browser to this URL to complete payment on the provider's hosted checkout page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Payment confirmed">
    The provider sends a webhook to Flexprice. Flexprice activates the subscription, finalizes the invoice, and marks the session `completed`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Automatic cleanup on failure">
    If the customer doesn't pay, cancels, or the session expires, all draft entities (subscription, invoice, payment) are automatically removed. No manual cleanup needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported providers and actions

| Payment provider | Available actions |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| Razorpay | `create_subscription` |

One-time payments, plan changes via Checkout, and additional payment providers are on the roadmap.

## Before you start

To use Checkout, you need:

1. A connected payment provider. For Razorpay, see [Setting up Razorpay](/integrations/razorpay/connection-setup).
2. The Razorpay webhook events configured for Checkout. See [Razorpay Checkout Setup](/docs/checkout/razorpay-checkout) for the exact list.
3. A customer already created in Flexprice. The `customer_external_id` in the session request must exist.
4. A plan with at least one non-zero charge. Checkout requires a non-zero invoice to generate a payment link.
