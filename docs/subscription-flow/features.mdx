## Overview

In FlexPrice, **Features are the foundation of your pricing and billing system**.  
A feature represents **what your customer consumes, accesses, or is entitled to**.

Before creating:

- Plans
- Subscriptions
- Usage-based billing

You **must** define features in the **Product Catalog**.

This guide walks you through **how to create a feature correctly**, explains **every required and important field**, and helps you understand how features behave in real-world billing systems.

---

## Prerequisites

Before creating a feature, ensure that:

- You have access to the **FlexPrice dashboard**
- You understand **what your product charges for**
  - Usage (API calls, transactions)
  - Limits (users, storage)
  - Access (modules, premium tools)

---

## Feature Creation Flow

1. Identify what is being consumed
2. Define how it is measured
3. Configure how usage is calculated
4. Save the feature for plan association

Each step depends on the previous one and must be completed in order.

  <Step title="Assign Subscription to Customer">
    Subscribe a customer to a plan to activate billing and feature access.
    
    **Path:**  
    `Customers â†’ Select Customer â†’ Add Subscription`
    
    ### Step-by-Step Process
    
    1. **Navigate to Customer**
       - Go to **Customers** dashboard
       - Click on the customer you want to create a subscription for
       - This opens the customer's details page
    
    2. **Initiate Subscription Creation**
       - On the customer details page, click **"Add Subscription"** button
       - This opens the subscription creation form
    
    3. **Select Plan**
       - Choose a pricing plan from the dropdown menu
       - Only **Active** plans are available for selection
       - The plan must have at least one feature attached
       
       >  **Note**: Ensure the plan is created and active before creating subscriptions.
    
    4. **Review Subscription Preview**
       - A preview panel appears on the right side showing:
         - **Plan Details**: Name, billing interval, currency
         - **Recurring Charges**: Fixed monthly/yearly fees
         - **Usage-Based Charges**: Metered features and pricing
         - **Total Estimated Cost**: Breakdown for the billing period
         - **Feature Entitlements**: Included features and limits
    
    5. **Configure Subscription Settings**
       
       **Required Fields:**
       
       - **Customer**: Pre-selected (the customer you're creating subscription for)
       
       - **Plan**: Select from available active plans
       
       - **Start Date**: When the subscription begins
         - Determines the first billing cycle
         - Can be set to today or a future date
         - Past dates are not allowed
       
       - **Billing Cycle Anchor**: The day of month/week when billing occurs
         - For monthly plans: Day 1-31 (e.g., 15th of each month)
         - For yearly plans: Month and day (e.g., January 15th)
         - Automatically calculated based on start date if not specified
       
       **Optional Fields:**
       
       - **End Date**: When the subscription should end
         - Default: **"Forever"** (ongoing subscription)
         - Set a specific date for time-limited subscriptions
         - Useful for trial periods or contract-based subscriptions
       
       - **Payment Method**: Link a payment method for automatic billing
         - Required if using automatic payment processing
         - Can be added later before first invoice
       
       - **Trial Period**: Enable free trial before billing starts
         - Set trial duration in days
         - Customer gets full access during trial
         - Billing starts after trial ends
    
    6. **Configure Override Line Items (Optional)**
       
       Customize pricing for this specific subscription:
       
       - **Override Amounts**: Modify charge amounts
       - **Override Quantities**: Adjust package quantities
       - **Override Billing Models**: Change pricing structure
       - **Override Tier Structures**: Modify volume-based tiers
       
       >  **Use Case**: Useful for custom enterprise deals or promotional pricing.
    
    7. **Apply Discounts (Optional)**
       
       - **Coupons**: Apply percentage or fixed-amount discounts
       - **Promotional Codes**: Time-bound discounts
       - **Custom Discounts**: Manual discount entry
       
       Discounts can be:
       - Percentage-based (e.g., 20% off)
       - Fixed amount (e.g., $50 off)
       - Time-bound (e.g., first 3 months)
    
    8. **Save Subscription**
       - Review all details in the preview panel
       - Click **"Add Subscription"** to create
       - Subscription appears in customer's subscription list
       - Status will be **"Active"** or **"Trialing"** (if trial enabled)
    
    ### Subscription States
    
    Subscriptions can be in different states:
    
    - **Trialing**: Free trial period is active
      - Customer has full access
      - No charges applied yet
      - Automatically transitions to Active after trial
    
    - **Active**: Subscription is active and billing
      - Customer has access to all plan features
      - Regular billing cycles occur
      - Invoices generated per billing schedule
    
    - **Past Due**: Payment failed or overdue
      - Customer access may be restricted
      - Retry attempts for payment
      - Can be reactivated after payment
    
    - **Canceled**: Subscription has been canceled
      - No future billing
      - Access continues until end of paid period
      - Can be reactivated if needed
    
    - **Paused**: Temporarily suspended
      - Billing paused
      - Access may be limited
      - Can be resumed
    
    ### Multiple Subscriptions
    
    **A single customer can have multiple active subscriptions:**
    
    - Each subscription operates independently
    - Different plans for different products/services
    - Separate billing cycles and invoices
    - Combined usage tracking across subscriptions
    - Example: Customer can have "Pro Plan" + "API Add-on" simultaneously
    
    ### Subscription Management
    
    After creation, you can:
    
    - **View Subscription Details**: See billing history, usage, and status
    - **Modify Subscription**: Upgrade, downgrade, or change plan
    - **Pause/Resume**: Temporarily suspend billing
    - **Cancel**: End subscription (immediate or end of period)
    - **Add Add-ons**: Attach additional features or services
    - **View Invoices**: See all generated invoices for this subscription
