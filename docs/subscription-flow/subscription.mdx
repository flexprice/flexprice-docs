## Overview

A **Subscription** connects a **Customer** to a **Plan** and starts billing.

---

## Create Subscription

**Path:**  
`Customers → Select Customer → Add Subscription`

---

## Required Fields

- **Customer**
- **Plan**
- **Start Date**
- **Billing Cycle Anchor**
- **Payment Method** (if applicable)

---

## Optional Components

### Charges
- One-time charges
- Recurring add-ons

### Discounts
- Percentage-based
- Fixed amount
- Time-bound

### Taxes
- GST / VAT
- Region-based rules

---

## Subscription States

- Trialing
- Active
- Past Due
- Canceled

---

```text
Feature → Plan → Customer → Subscription
