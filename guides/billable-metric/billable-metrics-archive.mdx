---
title: "Archive a billable metric"
---

1. Find your metric in the dashboard, click the **three-dot menu (⋮)**, and select **Archive**.
   <Frame>
     ![](/Screenshot2025-01-21at1.48.29AM.png)
   </Frame>

2. A confirmation pop-up appears where click on Archive to confirm that you no longer tracks new usage of that billable metric
   <Frame>
     ![](/Screenshot2025-01-21at1.49.07AM.png)
   </Frame>

3. Once archived, Status of the billable metric updates to **Inactive** and historical data remains accessible for reference.
   <Frame>
     ![](/Screenshot2025-01-21at1.51.32AM.png)
   </Frame>

4. You can’t “unarchive” a metric, but you can recreate a new one if needed.
